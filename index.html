FAQs About M Legasi

What is the monthly instalment for M Legasi? +
Based on 90% financing at 4.5% interest over 35 years, the estimated monthly instalment starts from approximately RM1,950 for the 20'×60' unit (RM635,000). Actual instalment depends on your bank's interest rate and loan tenure.
Is Semenyih too far from KL for daily commuting? +
M Legasi is 35km from KLCC, accessible via 5 major highways (LEKAS, SILK, SUKE, EKVE, PLUS). With the LEKAS toll exit just minutes away, typical drive time to KL is 40-50 minutes outside peak hours — comparable to many KL suburbs.
What's the rental potential near University of Nottingham? +
University of Nottingham Malaysia is just 4km from M Legasi, creating consistent rental demand from students, faculty, and staff. Freehold landed properties in university catchment areas historically command strong rental yields of 4-5%.
What is the difference between freehold and leasehold? +
Freehold means permanent ownership with no expiry — the land title belongs to you and your descendants forever. Leasehold typically runs for 99 years and may require costly renewal. M Legasi is fully freehold.
Does M Legasi have EV charging facilities? +
Yes. M Legasi is future-ready with dedicated EV charging points provided for homeowners, ensuring you're prepared for the shift to electric vehicles.
Can I get financing if I only have 10% down payment? +
Yes, most Malaysian banks offer up to 90% financing for freehold properties. Our sales team can connect you with preferred banking partners who offer competitive rates. Register above to learn more about available financing packages.
