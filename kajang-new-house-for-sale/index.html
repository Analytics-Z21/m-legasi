Houses for Sale near Kajang: Explore Nearby Landed Property Projects in Semenyih

M Legasi | December 17, 2024

Key Takeaways

  • Kajang Landed Property Appeal — Strategic location with excellent highway connectivity and proximity to essential amenities.
  • Semenyih as a Natural Extension — Offering similar conveniences with more space, greener surroundings, and competitive pricing.
  • Seamless Connectivity — Both townships connected via major highways, making Semenyih a practical alternative.
  • New Developments Near Kajang — M Legasi presents a 500-acre freehold township with modern living features.

Why Kajang Landed Property Appeals to Homebuyers

Kajang has long been a popular choice for homebuyers in Selangor, thanks to its well-established connectivity via Jalan Cheras, Grand Saga Expressway, and SILK Expressway. Over the years, more than ten new neighbourhoods have been developed, transforming Kajang into a thriving residential hub.

The township's strategic location near hospitals, medical centres, and commercial hubs makes it particularly attractive for families and professionals alike. However, as demand continues to rise, many buyers are now looking beyond Kajang to nearby areas that offer similar conveniences with more space and greener surroundings.

Semenyih, located just a short drive from Kajang, has emerged as a compelling alternative. It offers the same level of accessibility to essential amenities while providing a more peaceful, nature-oriented living environment.

Considering Alternatives Near Kajang for Your New Double Storey House

If you're searching for a house near Kajang, it's worth expanding your search to include Semenyih. Both Kajang and Semenyih have been gaining recognition as desirable residential areas in the Hulu Langat district, and for good reason.

Residents of Semenyih enjoy the same amenities available in Kajang, from shopping centres and schools to healthcare facilities. With both townships connected by well-maintained roads, moving between them is just a short drive away, making Semenyih a practical and rewarding choice for homebuyers.

Key Amenities to Consider When Buying a New Development Near Kajang in Semenyih

M Legasi embraces the '15-minute city' concept, where essential amenities are planned to be within a short drive from your doorstep. This means schools, shopping centres, healthcare facilities, and recreational spots are all conveniently accessible.

From SMK Bandar Rinching to Setia EcoHill Mall, lush parks to nearby hospitals, M Legasi's location ensures that residents never have to travel far for their daily needs. This thoughtful planning creates a self-sustaining community where convenience is built into the fabric of everyday life.

New Double Storey Landed House Near Kajang to Explore

M Legasi is Mah Sing's largest freehold township in the Klang Valley, spanning 500 acres with a Gross Development Value (GDV) of RM3.3 billion. The development is organised into three distinctive precincts — Impira, Adiya, and Embun — each designed to cater to different lifestyle preferences.

The township offers 2-storey terrace houses in a range of sizes, including 20'x60', 20'x65', and 20'x70' layouts. Each unit features 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, providing ample space for families to live comfortably.

Key features of M Legasi include:

  • Guarded community with 24/7 security patrols
  • 13-acre central park with lakes and landscaped gardens
  • Highway access via LEKAS (12km), SILK (26km), SKVE (38km), and PLUS (46km)
  • Freehold tenure for long-term ownership security

Explore Landed House Alternatives Near Kajang Today

Kajang and Semenyih are approximately 15-20 km apart, connected by the Kajang-Semenyih Highway. This proximity means that Semenyih residents can easily access all of Kajang's amenities while enjoying the benefits of a newer, more spacious township.

The Semenyih property market has shown strong momentum, with a median price of approximately RM628,000 and annual growth of 8.6%. For buyers seeking value and long-term appreciation, Semenyih presents an attractive proposition compared to more established — and more expensive — areas in Kajang.
