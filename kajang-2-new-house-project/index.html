Why Choose a Family-Focused Township When Buying a New Property in Kajang 2

M Legasi | September 25, 2025

As technology advances, many people are becoming more absorbed in their smart devices, which can weaken family bonds over time. At the same time, the pressures of city living and a lack of green spaces can make it harder to relax and reconnect with loved ones. That's why living in a property development designed around family life and togetherness has become more important than ever.

If you're thinking about buying a new property in the Kajang 2 area, here are some reasons why choosing a family-focused township might be the right move.

Spacious Landed Property Suitable for Families

Families naturally grow and change over time, and in a township designed with families in mind, developers often plan with this growth as a priority.

In such townships, houses tend to be spacious to offer enough room and flexibility for everyone. Typically, this means layouts with 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, as well as outdoor spaces for relaxation and family activities.

For instance, M Legasi by Mah Sing, located near the Kajang 2 area, was built with family living in mind. Beyond its spacious double-storey homes, it also provides sports and recreation facilities such as an outdoor gym, play lawn, breezy-torium, pickleball and badminton courts, and more.

New Project Near Amenities and Excellent Connectivity

It's important for families to live close to essential amenities, whether for children who attend school, elderly family members who may need nearby healthcare, or working professionals who commute daily.

That's why family-focused townships are often planned near supermarkets, schools, hospitals, shopping malls, places of worship, and other conveniences that support everyday living.

This applies to M Legasi as well. Beyond offering a family-friendly environment, M Legasi is strategically located with excellent connectivity to major highways such as LEKAS, SILK, SKVE, and PLUS.

M Legasi: A Landed Property Choice Close to Kajang 2

Located just about 7 km from Semenyih and close to Kajang 2, M Legasi offers a great alternative for those seeking a family-focused township. This development features freehold double-storey houses complemented by a vibrant commercial, lifestyle, and communal hub.

Beyond convenience, M Legasi places a strong emphasis on safety and quality of life. The township includes a guarded community with security patrols, as well as a beautiful 13-acre central park with lakes, all connected by green corridors.

Designed with modern living in mind, the homes here feature sustainable design, airy interiors, and a north-south orientation to reduce direct sunlight and improve comfort.

Create Better Family Moments in a New Kajang 2 Project

M Legasi's M Impira series offers double-storey homes with spacious layouts including:

  • Alidia: 20'x60' and 20'x65'
  • Feloria: 20'x70'
  • Astradia: 20'x60' and 20'x65'
  • Harmonia: 20'x70'

Each unit features en-suite bathrooms, family-centric living and dining spaces, a versatile under-stair area, and a spacious car porch.
