Is Buying a New House for Sale in Kajang Still Worth It in 2025?

September 25, 2025 M Legasi

Kajang continues to stand out as a promising investment location in 2025, supported by steady price appreciation and ongoing infrastructure development. As a mature town, Kajang offers excellent connectivity via the MRT Kajang Line and KTM Komuter, along with established schools, healthcare centres, and a generally lower cost of living compared to KL city centre. Many homebuyers consider Kajang because of its strategic location, making it a perennial favourite for those seeking value without sacrificing convenience.

Why It's Worth Buying a Landed House in Kajang

Kajang offers a good range of options for shopping, dining, and entertainment, making it a well-rounded township for families and working professionals alike. The town is well-connected to major transportation networks, ensuring residents can travel to and from KL with ease.

Key retail destinations in the area include MetroPoint, Plaza Metro Kajang, Ostia Kajang, Lotus's, and Giant Kajang, all of which provide everyday essentials as well as lifestyle and dining options. For those who commute, major highways such as SILK and LEKAS offer direct routes to neighbouring towns and the wider Klang Valley.

Green spaces and recreational areas are also part of Kajang's appeal. Parks like Taman Cergas and Kajang Utama Mini Park give families room to unwind, jog, or enjoy weekend outings. And of course, Kajang is famous for its diverse food choices, from the iconic satay Kajang to a wide variety of local and international cuisines that line the town's bustling streets.

Another New Property in the Maturing Township Around Kajang

While Kajang itself remains a popular choice, neighbouring areas are also gaining significant attention from homebuyers and investors. One township that has risen in prominence is Semenyih, located just a short drive from Kajang town centre. Semenyih's appeal is supported by its natural attractions, including the well-known Broga Hill, Setia EcoHill Park, and Sungai Gabai waterfalls, all of which contribute to a more relaxed, nature-oriented lifestyle.

Among the developments making waves in Semenyih is M Legasi by Mah Sing, a project that offers freehold landed homes set within green, gated communities. This development has attracted buyers looking for spacious living in a secure environment, without straying too far from the conveniences that Kajang and the wider Klang Valley provide.
